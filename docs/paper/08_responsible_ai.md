# Responsible AI

FInk must remain a local-first review-priority system. It must not provide legal
advice or a legal/fraud/validity verdict. It must preserve authority gating,
uncertainty behavior, no-invented-number behavior, Korean-canonical evidence,
privacy boundaries, and synthetic-data limits.

The FINK-S8-03 release posture is conservative: HR-01 and HR-02 are satisfied by
not publishing current-law or authoritative webtoon-law claims, HR-03/05/06 are
satisfied by canonical-Korean, date-stamped, and invariant-checked outputs, HR-07
is resolved by the 2026 Spring IE412 AI for Finance deadline decision, and HR-08
remains a human academic-integrity attestation recorded in gate metadata rather
than generated by the system (`CLM-S8-HR-DISPOSITION`).
