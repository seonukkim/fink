# Discussion and Limitations

## Course Mapping

This note covers the discussion part of the course report
(`CLM-S7-MAP-DISCUSSION`).

## Result Scope

The measured values in `06_results.md` are limited to synthetic/sanitized local
fixtures. They support implementation and evaluation sanity checks, not
generalized deployment performance, validated scoring weights, or a legal
conclusion (`CLM-S7-RESULT-SCOPE`).

## Open Human Gates

Current-law verification, webtoon-specific official extraction, sensitive
bilingual legal-alias sign-off, dated-figure verification, public release
approval, and AI-use attestation remain human-gated. Until those gates are
closed, the paper must keep evidence date-stamped or unverified where
applicable and avoid current-law claims.

## Method Limits

Scoring weights and thresholds are heuristic. The DFL-inspired evaluation
framing helps organize decision-relevant metrics, but it is not evidence of
end-to-end DFL training or validated financial-loss prediction
(`CLM-S7-METHOD-DFL`).
