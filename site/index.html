FInk

Evidence-grounded financial review priority for creator contracts.

FInk is a local-first project for prioritizing financially consequential contract clauses. This page is static, public-safe, and synthetic-demo only.

FInk reports Contractual Financial Review Priority only. It is not legal advice and not a fraud, illegality, validity, unfairness, or guaranteed-loss verdict.

Public page data is synthetic or ledger-derived. No real contracts, private-book text, long official excerpts, keys, or private runtime inputs are included.

Method shape

Four dimensions stay separate

Review Priority

An ordinal Contractual Financial Review Priority signal for deciding which clauses deserve closer human review.

Monetary Exposure Range

Low, base, and high scenarios from extracted values or explicit user-editable synthetic assumptions.

Time Exposure

Payment timing, recoupment duration, contract term, renewal windows, and delay pathways are shown as typed fields.

Evidence and OCR Confidence

Confidence stays separate from the priority score, so missing or unclear data raises uncertainty instead of inventing values.
